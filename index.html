Welcome to Dublin Coding!

Hello future Dublin Coders! First you can take a breath taking look at our curriculum for 8 classes for a introductory rate of $5 per class with the first 2 classes free. According to our curriculum Dublin Coding offers HTML to start off the 3 classes, the the students will move on to make their websites and games interactive for their players/viewers. After fully creating a interactive website, they continue to python game development. Along with all the classes are exciting projects to help the students get exposure to the lesson by trying it out themselves.
